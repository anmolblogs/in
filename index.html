Welcome !


I'm Anmol.
I'm an Electronics Enthusiast and Tech Geek. I love to make electronics projects. I also love to repair and reverse-engineer broken electronics circuits/devices. Here on this website, I post blogs which cover my journey of making these projects. I also post about my thoughts and ideas on certain other techy and non-techy topics.



Why this website ???

The simplest answer to this question is that I wanted a way by which I could share my ideas and thoughts not just with my family and friends but with the whole world and hence "stand out in crowd".


Why not other platforms ???

Indeed, there are a lot of platforms to share stuff globally; for example : YouTube, Facebook, Twitter, Reddit, Blogger, etc... I even made a YouTube channel back in 2019 with a somewhat "cool-looking" intro but soon realized that video-making is not my thing. I also tried some other popular sites but none of them was of my taste.

I wanted a platform which was :

  1. Simple
  2. Customizable
  3. Non-time consuming
  4. Non-distracting

And I found out that I could build my very own website and it will fullfill all these characterstics that I wanted.

Hence, here it is !
